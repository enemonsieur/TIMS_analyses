================================================================================
EXP08 ARTIFACT FILTERING SUMMARY: Raw vs. SASS vs. SSD
================================================================================

ANALYSIS OVERVIEW:
  Compared three spatial filter paths for stimulus artifact suppression:
    1. Raw: Fixed channel locked at 10% intensity (best SNR)
    2. SASS: Covariance-based artifact subtraction (ON − late-OFF)
    3. SSD: Signal-dominance eigendecomposition (signal vs. view bands)

RANKING CRITERION: SNR (signal-band power / view-band power, linear ratio)

TIME WINDOWS:
    ON: -0.5 to 1.0 s (stimulus present, full epoch)
    ITPC/PLV: -0.5 to 1.0 s (phase locking analysis window)
    late-OFF: 1.5 to 3.2 s (noise reference)

FREQUENCY BANDS:
    Signal band: 12.5–13.5 Hz (stimulus fundamental)
    View band: 4.0–20.0 Hz (broadband reference)
