PROBLEM 1: RAW CHANNEL RE-SELECTION PER INTENSITY
  Issue:    Best raw EEG channel selected independently at each intensity
  Impact:   Can't distinguish artifact contamination from spatial variation
  Solution: Lock best channel at 10%, reuse across all intensities
  Status:   ✅ SOLVED

PROBLEM 2: SASS + HIDDEN SSD MIXING
  Issue:    SASS path applied secondary eigendecomposition on cleaned data
  Impact:   Violates principle that SASS and SSD are separate pipelines
  Solution: Pure SASS only; rank all cleaned channels by PLV (not power ratio)
  Status:   ✅ SOLVED

PROBLEM 3: SSD RANKED BY WRONG METRIC
  Issue:    SSD components ranked by broadband power ratio (proxy)
  Impact:   Inconsistent with raw path (uses PLV); indirect metric
  Solution: Rank SSD components by PLV to GT (direct metric)
  Status:   ✅ SOLVED

================================================================================
METHODOLOGY: THE CORE INSIGHT
================================================================================

OLD PIPELINE: Asymmetric Selection Criteria
  Raw:  Selected by max PLV (direct)
  SASS: Selected by min power_ratio (proxy)
  SSD:  Selected by min power_ratio (proxy)

  Problem: Can't fairly compare; different selection logic

NEW PIPELINE: Symmetric Selection Criteria
  Raw:  Selected by max PLV (direct) — fixed at 10%
  SASS: Selected by max PLV (direct) — per intensity
  SSD:  Selected by max PLV (direct) — per intensity

  Benefit: All three use same metric; fair, direct comparison
  Interpretation: Differences in PLV reflect artifact/signal effects,
                  not spatial variation or selection logic
